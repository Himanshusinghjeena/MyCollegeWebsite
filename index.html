Course We Offer

Explore our programs designed to nurture your passion for technology and equip you with the skills to shape the future.

BCA

The Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program offers a solid foundation in computer science, software development, and IT fundamentals. It prepares students for roles in programming, database management, and application development across various industries, while also encouraging logical thinking and problem-solving skills essential for todayâ€™s digital world.

MCA

The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) is a two-year postgraduate program that deepens knowledge in advanced computing, software engineering, and data structures. It enables students to pursue careers in software development, systems analysis, cybersecurity, and cloud computing, equipping them with strong technical and project management skills for the IT industry.

BBA

The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) is a three-year undergraduate program covering marketing, finance, and human resource management. It fosters essential managerial and leadership skills, encouraging strategic thinking, entrepreneurship, and effective communication. This program prepares students for business environments and offers a pathway to higher studies like MBA or PGDM.

MBA

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a globally recognized program that enhances leadership, strategic management, and analytical skills. Students specialize in areas such as marketing, finance, operations, and human resources. The program prepares graduates for high-impact careers in corporate leadership, entrepreneurship, consultancy, and global business management roles.

PGDM

The Post Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) is a dynamic, industry-oriented program focusing on real-world business challenges. It includes specializations in marketing, finance, operations, and HR, supported by case studies, internships, and workshops. The PGDM curriculum is designed to nurture effective decision-makers and visionary business leaders for the modern world.

B.TECH

The Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) is a four-year program that provides in-depth knowledge in core engineering fields such as computer science, electronics, mechanical, and civil. It blends theoretical foundations with hands-on experience, enabling students to design, innovate, and implement technology-driven solutions across industries including IT, manufacturing, and infrastructure.

M.TECH

The Master of Technology (M.Tech) is a specialized postgraduate program offering advanced study in specific engineering domains. It emphasizes research, development, and technical expertise. With a focus on innovation and practical problem-solving, the program prepares students for careers in research, academia, high-tech industries, and leadership roles in engineering sectors.
